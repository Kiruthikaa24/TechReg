Play with py is dedicated to revive the geek in you and to provide a great battle ground for programmers who dream of making it big in the world of programming. Play with py is a website that helps in improving coding skills in many programming languages. At Play with py we aim to create a global community that believes in friendly competition and consistent learning.

Instructions:

  • Selection is purely based on performance in the preliminary round.
